Official Rules of rdvptrwjeiptwpes

1. Objective of the Game

In rdvptrwjeiptwpes, the goal is to accumulate the most points by playing specific cards and forming temporary alliances with other players.

2. Setup

  • Each player starts with 6 cards.
  • The central board has 2 discard zones and 1 draw zone.
  • Bonus tokens are placed in the center of the table.

3. Turn Structure

  1. Draw phase: each player draws 1 card.
  2. Action phase: play up to 2 cards (attack, defense, bluff...).
  3. Negotiation phase: form or break alliances, trade cards with allies.
  4. Resolution phase: tally points, apply card effects.

4. End of Game

The game ends when a player reaches 50 points or when there are no more cards to draw. The player with the most points is declared the winner.
